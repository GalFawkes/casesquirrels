Thank you for visiting!

Welcome to the website for Case Squirrels, in honor of the lovable squirrels on campus. When you think of squirrels, what do you think of? Acorns? Springtime? Leaves in the trees? Little pawprints dotting the snow? Or perhaps, you just think of the squirrels themselves. We are here to remind you of the beauty and joy that comes with admiring all the different squirrels on campus. And do not lie to us — we know you have all thought about petting one in the past. Their fur coats just look so soft! We do not blame you for thinking that.

        In fact, maybe you are wondering if it is true. Maybe you have a lot of questions about squirrels around campus, or about squirrels in general. That is where we come in. Don’t hesitate to reach out to our phone number for a quick answer to all your squirrel-related questions. I mean, we put that number on our website for a reason. Just call!

        What would be the point of putting a phone number on our website if no one looked at it, just a little? If no one looked at how nicely those numbers fit into place next to each other, and how many different meanings could be found within those digits? I mean, who wouldn’t consider the infinite complexities of such a wonderful string of numerals! I know I would consider it. WHOLEHEARTEDLY. Yup. That’s what I would do. And, in case you haven’t seen it on the website, it’s 555-6887.

        But I digress. What is meant by all of this is, we are here to answer your questions and take your recommendations regarding squirrel-related adventures. There is no idea too crazy, no question too confusing. We would be nuts to say otherwise!
